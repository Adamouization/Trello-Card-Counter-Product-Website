Trello Card Counter

An elegant and free browser extension that directly integrates into any Trello board to count the total number of cards in each list and across the whole board.


  • Learn More

Quick & Easy to use

Simply download the extension on your favourite browser (Chrome and Safari coming soon), and open it on any of your Trello boards to view number of cards per list, total cards across the board and average number of cards per list!

  • Download Now
